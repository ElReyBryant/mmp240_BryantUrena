Spaghetti Recipe

This is a simple spaghetti recipe! with common ingredients, dicently quick to prepare and serve to mutiple people!.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add a pinch of salt, and cook the noodles according to package instructions. Then Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add chopped onion and garlic; sauté until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
